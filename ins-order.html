Instructions

Your task is to remember the order that the letters are presented.

Please remain alert as the letters will be flashed very quickly for 1 second only.

After 7 letter are presented, please drag and drop your answer at the response page to match the order they are presented.

If you made an error in the matching, please click the “Delete” button to remove your response.

After you are done with your response, please click continue to proceed with the next trial.

You will be given 5 practice trials to familiarise with the task.

At the beginning of each trial, a ‘!’ will indicate the start of the trial.

If you have any question, please raise your hand and an experimenter will attend to you shortly.

Click on Continue to begin the practice sessions.
