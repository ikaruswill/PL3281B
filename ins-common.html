Dear participants,

Please read the instruction carefully before proceeding.

In this experiment, you will be shown 7 letters (one flashed at a time) and will be asked to do a matching task after the letters are shown. There will be a total of 24 trials. During the presentation of the letters, you may hear sound inputs from the earphones. You are to ignore the sounds and continue with the task. By clicking “Proceed”, you indicate that you are willing to participate in this experiment. Should you feel uncomfortable in the middle of the experiment and wish to withdraw, please raise your hand to notify the experimenter.

Thank you.
